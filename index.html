About Us

Compressor Ai was born out of my fervent love for photography and the realization that there was a need for a quick and dependable image compression solution. As a dedicated student at Holberton School, I saw an opportunity to create this project as part of my coursework.

By combining my passion for photography and my programming skills, I was able to design Compressor Ai as a tool that provides users with a seamless image compression experience. With a simple and intuitive interface, this software can reduce the size of images without sacrificing quality, making it an ideal solution for photographers, graphic designers, and anyone who wants to optimize their website's loading times.
